   Question
   Did I/You/She/He/It/We/They walk/go/come?

   and irregular.
   Did you go? Did you walk? 
   Negative
   Subject did not (didn't) walk. go. come.
   Subject + did not + infinitive for all persons and all verbs,
   regular and irregular.
